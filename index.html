Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed for property verification?

Typically, we require the Sale Deed, Index II, 7/12 Extract, Property Tax receipts, and Commencement/Completion certificates. We can guide you through the full checklist during consultation.

How long does the Notary process take?

Basic Notary services can usually be completed on the same day if all parties are present with valid original identification documents.

Do I need an appointment for consultation?

While walk-ins are possible, we recommend booking an appointment via Call or WhatsApp to ensure the Advocate is available to give you dedicated time.
